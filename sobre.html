About the Author

The account @young.with.money on TikTok exists to show you that it's possible to build wealth even with a low income. Here, you’ll find straightforward content about money, personal finance, and income generation, all focused on the reality of those starting their financial journey — interns, college students, young workers.

In addition to practical tips and strategies to save money, the profile also sells eBooks and digital products created to help you get out of tight spots, learn how to manage money, and start profiting from what you know.

Our goal is to simplify financial education and prove that with the right strategies, any young person can transform their financial situation, even starting from zero.

Mission

To democratize access to financial education for youth, showing that it is possible to build wealth regardless of starting salary.

Values

  • Financial transparency
  • Accessible education
  • Focus on practical results
  • Collaborative community
